Hey digital flyers, my name is Jacob Blanco. I'm a PhD student in particle physics at RHUL in the UK. If you care I'm working as part of the Top group for the ATLAS collaboration at CERN. If you're a parent, check out CERN for kids; it's a nice resource for children to learn about the amazing work being done at CERN.

I decided to make this blog because my brain explodes every day with things I want to talk (rant) about and share with people. I think we live in a wonderful world and sharing our experiences and knowledge with others is a fantastic way of exploring it.
